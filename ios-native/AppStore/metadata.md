## Description

STICKDEATH ∞ is the ultimate platform for stick figure combat animations — a powerful creation studio meets a thriving community, all in one app.

Whether you're a seasoned animator or picking up your first digital pen, STICKDEATH ∞ gives you everything you need to create brutal, beautiful stick figure fight scenes frame by frame.

POWERFUL ANIMATION STUDIO
• 12 professional brushes — pen, marker, spray, charcoal, neon, pixel, and more
• Rig & bone system with IK solver for natural stick figure posing
• Frame-by-frame timeline with onion skinning and up to 30 FPS playback
• Multi-layer support — separate your characters, backgrounds, and effects
• Fill tool, lasso select, shapes, and text for complete creative control
• Sound effects library — punches, explosions, whooshes, and ambient tracks
• Import photos and videos directly into your animation timeline
• 75+ background scenes — cyberpunk cities, dark forests, medieval castles
• Export to MP4 with custom watermark and audio mixing

AI-POWERED CREATIVITY
• Spatter AI assistant — get animation tips, feedback on your work, and creative ideas
• AI background generation — describe a scene, get a custom backdrop

COMMUNITY & CHALLENGES
• Browse a curated feed of the latest and greatest animations
• Weekly challenges with featured creator prizes
• Follow your favorite animators and build your audience
• Real-time community chat — share tips, ask questions, get inspired
• Comment, react, and battle for supremacy on the leaderboard

FREE & PRO
• Free tier — full studio access, community features, and unlimited projects
• Pro ($4.99/month) — AI tools, priority exports, exclusive brushes, no watermark

Built by animators, for animators. The spirit of the original StickDeath.com reborn for a new generation.
